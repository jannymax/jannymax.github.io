How a session works

1

Watch the story

Kiki and friends come to life in a short Lottie animation. Watch it through once to follow the plot.

2

Record each line

The app plays the model voice, then waits. Your child taps to record their own take — at their own pace.

3

Replay your dub

Hit play and hear the full story told in your child's voice, synced to the animation.

4

Keep it forever

Save to My Works or export a video to your photo library as a keepsake.
